Introduction

Through Earth's geologic time, the atmosphere went through many changes.

Earth History

Archean Eon

Earth's early atmosphere was anoxic and mainly consisted of water vapor, carbon dioxide, nitrogen, carbon monoxide, hydrogen sulfied, and hydrogen chloride. The Earth's atmosphere became oxygenated by the Proterozoic, which began 2.5 billion years ago.

Geologic Time Scale

The Archean Eon which began 4.6 billion years ago, is the oldest unit of time on the geological time scale.
